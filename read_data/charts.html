Race Summary

EMPIRE KNIGHT settled off the leaders early, moved far wide through the second turn, reached the fore with a furlong to run and edged clear late. LOS BORRACHOS lacked early speed, raced wide through the second turn, angled out further into the stretch and finished with a late rally. TOWN EXTENSION raced off the pacesetters early, moved extremely wide through the second turn, reached the leaders into the stretch and gave way late. DOWNEY GAP pressed the pace from the outside, moved up to take a slight edge nearing the quarter and gave way in the drive. MARKET OUTLOOK chased the leaders from the outside, angled out in mid-stretch and finished evenly late. POWERFUL INSTINCT was void of early foot, saved ground through the second turn, angled out nearing the eighth pole and finished with a late bid. SUNSET DISTRICT was slow to begin, raced wide through the second turn, angled out further into the stretch and found his best stride late. ETON BLUE tracked the pace of KULBOYZ from the inside and gradually weakened. LORD TRONDOR chased the leaders from the outside for six furlongs and weakened. KULBOYZ set a pressured pace from the inside for three-quarters and weakened.
